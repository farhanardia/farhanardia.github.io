WE ARE KUL KUL.

A food and beverages company with a goal to bring freshness towards
the community in a unique way while preserving Indonesia's
authentic taste on our products.
What are we trying
to achieve?
We have a vision to make an online-based
culinary company with Indonesia recognized
quality ingredient.

To get there, we required to complete our
mission ranging from serving refreshing
food and beverages to making a viral trend
where people can accept unique inovation
while preserving Indonesia's authentic taste.
  • Serving refreshing food and beverages.
  • Making a viral trend where people can
    accept unique inovation while preserving
    Indonesia's authentic taste.
  • Inovating products that consumers need
    without sacrificing quality.
  • Always use top quality ingredient.
  • Make a healthy and nutrious foods or
    drinks that everyone can enjoy.
What does our logo
means?

Iceberg represents freshness that we try
to deliver to you through our products. With five
different colors representing our company member.


Kul Kul is a pun for the word "cool",
indicating that we want to give you the pleasing
cool sensation when you eat or drink our products.

Mangkok Ayam Jago represents Indonesian's
authentic taste exists on our products.
